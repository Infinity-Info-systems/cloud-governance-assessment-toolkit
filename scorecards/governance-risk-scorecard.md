# Governance Risk Scorecard

## Purpose

Capture the risks created when governance controls are weak, unclear, or not being followed.

## Risk Categories

- decision risk
- policy risk
- compliance risk
- delivery risk
- operational risk

## Risk Rating Guidance

| Rating | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Low | Limited impact and low likelihood |
| Medium | Noticeable gap that should be tracked |
| High | Business-significant gap requiring action |
| Critical | Immediate exposure needing escalation |

## Example Findings

- unclear approval paths for architecture exceptions
- lack of periodic policy review
- inconsistent evidence for control operation
- no formal escalation path for repeated exceptions

## Use

Use this scorecard to capture governance failures that increase operational or compliance exposure.
