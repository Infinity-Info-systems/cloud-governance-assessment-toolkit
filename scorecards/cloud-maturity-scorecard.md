# Cloud Maturity Scorecard

## Purpose

Measure how consistently cloud governance, control execution, and operating discipline are applied.

## Scoring Areas

- decision rights
- policy enforcement
- platform standards
- exception management
- measurement and reporting

## Example Interpretation

| Score | Interpretation |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | No shared cloud governance model |
| 2 | Partial ownership and inconsistent standards |
| 3 | Documented controls with uneven adoption |
| 4 | Measured and repeatable operating model |
| 5 | Optimized and continuously improved model |

## Evidence To Gather

- governance charter
- architecture standards
- policy exceptions
- control reports
- review meeting outputs
