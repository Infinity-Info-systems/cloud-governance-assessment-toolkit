# Reliability Scorecard

## Purpose

Assess how well the operating model supports stable services, fast recovery, and useful observability.

## Scoring Areas

- service ownership
- incident handling
- alert quality
- error budget discipline
- recovery readiness

## Evidence To Collect

- incident summaries
- alert routing rules
- SLO reports
- post-incident reviews
- recovery test results

## Example Interpretation

- low score: reactive operations with limited measurement
- mid score: defined processes with mixed adoption
- high score: measured reliability with active improvement loops

## Use

Use this scorecard to compare how well services are operating, recovering, and improving over time.
