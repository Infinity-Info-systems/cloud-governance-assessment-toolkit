# Assessment Dashboard

## Leadership Summary

- total domains assessed
- average maturity score
- high-priority findings
- remediation progress
- confidence by domain

## Suggested Views

- score by domain
- risk by domain
- confidence by finding
- action status by owner
- trend over time

## Dashboard Table

| Widget | Purpose | Audience |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Domain score heat map | Show where the largest gaps sit | Executives and governance leads |
| Risk trend chart | Show whether risk is improving | Executives and program owners |
| Action backlog | Show what is still open | Delivery leads |
| Confidence summary | Show where evidence is weak | Assessment reviewers |

## Dashboard Intent

Use the dashboard to show the overall assessment picture quickly and make the next decision obvious.

## Decision Questions

- where is the biggest exposure?
- which remediation removes the most risk?
- which items need leadership action?
- what should be re-assessed next cycle?
