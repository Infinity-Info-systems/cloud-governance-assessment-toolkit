# Assessment Dashboard

## Leadership Summary

- total domains assessed
- average maturity score
- high-priority findings
- remediation progress
- confidence by domain

## Suggested Views

- score by domain
- risk by domain
- confidence by finding
- action status by owner
- trend over time

## Dashboard Intent

Use the dashboard to show the overall assessment picture quickly and make the next decision obvious.

## Decision Questions

- where is the biggest exposure?
- which remediation removes the most risk?
- which items need leadership action?
- what should be re-assessed next cycle?
