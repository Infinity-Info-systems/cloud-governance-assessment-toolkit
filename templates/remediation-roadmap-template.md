## Sequencing Guidance

- fix the highest-risk control gaps first
- remove blockers before broadening scope
- start with actions that unlock multiple domains
- keep each phase small enough to track

## Status Model

- blocked
- completed

## Review Cadence

- weekly for active remediation
- monthly for executive review
- quarterly for re-assessment

## Roadmap Rule

Keep the roadmap short enough to manage and specific enough to hold owners accountable.
