# Governance Maturity Model

## Model Stages

### Stage 1: Ad Hoc

- practices are informal
- ownership is unclear
- evidence is sparse

### Stage 2: Emerging

- some policies and reviews exist
- adoption is uneven
- exceptions are not well managed

### Stage 3: Defined

- governance practices are documented
- teams know the process
- execution is still inconsistent

### Stage 4: Managed

- controls are measured
- owners are accountable
- review cycles are recurring

### Stage 5: Optimized

- governance is continuously improved
- metrics drive decisions
- operating model changes are deliberate and evidence-based

## Maturity Signals

- formal decision rights
- policy lifecycle management
- control attestations
- exception tracking
- remediation follow-through

## Use

Use this model to understand whether governance is still ad hoc or already operating as a repeatable management practice.
