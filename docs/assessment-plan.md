# Assessment Plan

This plan turns the cloud governance toolkit into a repeatable assessment workflow.

## Purpose

Use this plan to collect evidence, score maturity, and produce a remediation roadmap for cloud governance, SRE, DR, FinOps, and security controls.

## Plan Goals

- create one repeatable assessment flow
- make findings traceable to evidence
- produce a clear executive view
- generate a sequenced remediation plan
- support re-assessment after improvement work

## Assessment Phases

### 1. Scope

- define in-scope business units and platforms
- identify the assessment owners
- confirm time window and evidence sources
- record the business driver for the assessment
- identify any audit, regulatory, or executive deadlines

### 2. Evidence Collection

- questionnaires
- checklists
- interviews
- dashboards and reports
- policy and control documents
- architecture diagrams and operating models
- incident and change records
- DR test evidence
- spend and forecast reports for FinOps
- exception logs and attestation records

### 3. Scoring

- score each domain against the toolkit model
- record the current state and target state
- capture confidence level and gaps
- separate maturity from risk so the result stays actionable
- note whether evidence is direct, indirect, or self-reported
- identify the most material gap in each domain

### 4. Findings Review

- validate findings with stakeholders
- rank risks by impact and urgency
- identify quick wins and structural issues
- confirm ownership for every high-priority finding
- record disagreements and the evidence needed to resolve them
- prepare the executive narrative before the final review

### 5. Remediation Planning

- assign owners
- sequence actions by dependency
- define target dates
- map actions into a roadmap
- group actions into 30, 60, and 90 day horizons
- separate policy, process, tooling, and operating-model changes
- identify items that require funding or leadership decisions

## Example Assessment Output

| Domain | Score | Gap | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cloud Governance | 3/5 | Policy execution consistency | High |
| SRE Maturity | 2/5 | Alert quality and service ownership | High |
| DR Readiness | 3/5 | Test frequency and evidence | Medium |
| FinOps Readiness | 2/5 | Tagging and commitment governance | High |
| Security Compliance | 4/5 | Exception handling discipline | Medium |

## Required Deliverables

- assessment scope note
- evidence register
- domain scorecards
- executive summary
- remediation roadmap
- decision log
- re-assessment recommendation

## Recommended Actions

- use one questionnaire set across all domains
- produce one executive summary for leadership
- keep the remediation roadmap separate from the findings log
- revisit scores after remediation cycles
- keep the raw evidence register for future comparison
- write recommendations as concrete actions, not abstract principles
- make the roadmap reviewable by both executives and implementers
